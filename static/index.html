Philosopher's Football

Board

Rules

  • Board is 19x15
  • Goal is to get white stone over opponent's goal line
  • Players alternate turns
  • Placing a black stone counts as a turn
  • Moving the white stone counts as a turn

Help

Player 1, it is your turn!

Remember, your goal is to move the white stone to Row 19 or above.

Player 2, it is your turn!

Remember, your goal is to move the white stone to Row 1 or below.
